My name is Paul Brooks I have been practicing marital arts since 1992(24yrs now) I first started JKD with Carruthers martial arts in 1996 and was given permission to start a small group in 2009. We are one of only two official groups in England under Tommy Carruthers. I currently hold the rank advance level 2(Group Leader/coach) with the Intercepting Fist Organisation, a global group dedicated to the preservation and teaching of Jeet Kune do as it was intended by the founder Bruce Lee.

Tommy Carruthers is considered today to be the world’s leading instructor/practitioner of Bruce Lees martial art and is often called upon by both Bruce’s direct family and students to demonstrate the art or conduct seminars in JKD world-wide. His own teachers were Bruce’s top students, Jesse Glover(Seattle), Ted Wong(LA), Howard Williams(Oakland) and Leo Fong, Bruce’s friend and training partner. More info here: www.tommycarruthers.com
